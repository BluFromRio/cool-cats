My dad is Mexican, and José Hernández is an inspiring Mexican-American tech hero. From working in the fields as a child to becoming a NASA astronaut, Hernández embodies resilience, perseverance, and the power of education. He is a model for intersectionality, showing how diverse backgrounds enrich STEM fields. His journey proves that with dedication, anyone can reach for the stars.
