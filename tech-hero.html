Meet Our Tech Heroes

Inspirations that have shaped our journeys in technology.

Ben’s Tech Hero: Fabrice Bellard

My tech hero is Fabrice Bellard because his commitment to open-source software has made powerful tools accessible to people around the world, regardless of their background or resources. Through projects like QEMU, FFmpeg, and TinyCC, he has helped democratize technology, proving that innovation isn’t just for large corporations but for anyone with curiosity and drive. His work ensures that underprivileged communities, students, and independent developers can access cutting-edge tools, breaking down barriers in STEM and empowering more people to create, experiment, and learn.

Hani’s Tech Hero: Annie Easley

My tech hero is Annie Easley because she broke barriers in the field of computer science and made significant contributions to NASA's space programs. As a Black woman interested in the cybersecurity field, she has been an inspiration to me, showing that determination and talent can lead to groundbreaking achievements, regardless of the challenges one may face.

Tristan’s Tech Hero: José Hernández

My dad is from México, and José Hernández is an inspiring example of perseverance and innovation in the Méxican community. As an engineer and astronaut, he has broken barriers in STEM, proving that dedication and hard work can lead to incredible achievements. His journey is a testament to the importance of representation in tech and science, showing how diverse voices and experiences strengthen these fields.
