Tristan's Hero

José Hernández

My dad is Mexican, and José Hernández is a true role model for the Mexican community and beyond. From working in the fields as a child to becoming a NASA astronaut and engineer, he embodies perseverance, hard work, and the power of education. His story is a testament to how diverse backgrounds strengthen the tech and science fields. He is a symbol of intersectionality, breaking barriers and inspiring future generations.
